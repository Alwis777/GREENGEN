Redefining Farming in Sri Lanka

GreenGen is an innovative mobile application designed to empower Sri Lankan farmers by facilitating the adoption of modern greenhouse technology. We bridge critical gaps in knowledge, access, and finance to foster a resilient and prosperous agricultural sector.

Knowledge Empowerment

Providing accessible, localized information on advanced farming techniques.

Economic Growth

Opening new avenues for market access, financial support, and quality supplies.

Environmental Stewardship

Promoting sustainable practices that adapt to climate change and enhance food security.

The Hurdles Facing Sri Lankan Farmers

Climatic Volatility & Declining Yields

Unpredictable weather patterns, rising temperatures, and traditional farming methods lead to reduced crop output and economic instability.

Barriers to Modernization

Lack of capital for greenhouse investments, limited access to modern agricultural R&D, and an absence of vital grants and microloans.

Information & Resource Fragmentation

Farmers struggle with disparate, often unreliable, information sources for best practices, real-time data, and vetted suppliers.

GreenGen: Our Integrated Solution

Smart Knowledge Hub

Empowered Commerce

A transparent marketplace for quality inputs and a financial hub for investment opportunities.

AI-Driven Support

Instant diagnosis and a collaborative community for peer-to-peer and expert assistance.

Key Features that Transform Farming

Comprehensive Crop & Greenhouse Library 📚

Access detailed, localized guides in Sinhala and Tamil for greenhouse construction and optimal crop management. Our premium tier offers real-time, location-specific environmental recommendations.

Empowering farmers with the precise knowledge needed for success, tailored to their specific context.

Verified Marketplace & Financial Hub 💰

A secure online marketplace featuring supplier ratings and price comparison for high-quality equipment and fertilizers. Our integrated Financial Hub provides listings of grants and microloans specifically for greenhouse farming investments in Sri Lanka.

Connecting farmers with trusted resources and crucial financial support to kickstart and sustain their modern ventures.

Premium Tier Advantage: Unlock exclusive discounts of up to 60% off on initial essential greenhouse supplies.

AI-Powered Crop Doctor 🔬 & Dynamic Community Forum

Instantly diagnose plant diseases through image analysis with our AI Crop Doctor, receiving immediate treatment recommendations in English, Sinhala, or Tamil. Engage with a vibrant Community Forum for peer support, knowledge exchange, and expert consultation.

Providing immediate, intelligent support and fostering a collaborative ecosystem for continuous learning and problem-solving.
